What makes us become famous!

BRIGHTWORLD is a company that focuses on the design and manufacture of LED bulbs. We offerprofessional OEM/ODM/SKD LED solutions and branding/marketing support to clients. The company was founded in August,2015 in VIETNAM, and now becomes a top exporter of LED bulbs in SAIGON, selling to over 50 countries worldwide.

We are the LED Bulb Expert because we de vote our 5 years of global experience in the lighting industry into the development of LED bulb products. Our SAGOLI(SAIGONLIGHT) certified Witnessed Testing Lab is focusing solely on the research and development of more energy-efficient LEDs.

We were awarded with the “SAIGON FAMOUS EXPORTING ENTERPRISE” and “AAA ENTERPRISE” by SAIGON Customs. We are among the first group of strategic partners with (DIENQUANG, PHILIPS, SSLIGHT AND PANASONIC) in August, 2017.
